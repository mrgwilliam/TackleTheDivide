About Tackling The Divide

Tackling The Divide is a platform dedicated to raising awareness about the Digital Divide, offering information, innovative ideas, and valuable resources for individuals committed to propagating our mission.

The website was established by Guillermo Rodriguez, a student at the University of Colorado Boulder, as part of coursework for Social Movements class.

Please note that the content presented on this site is not endorsed by the University of Colorado Boulder and is not affiliated with the University.

Note: The images on this site are created using generative AI (Midjourney and Adobe Firefly) the prompts are provided below the images and AI used.
